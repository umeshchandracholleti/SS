Product Description

The 3M H-701R-UV Industrial Safety Helmet is designed for maximum protection in hazardous work environments. Built with high-density polyethylene (HDPE), this helmet provides superior impact resistance and durability.

Key Features:

  • ISI certified for Indian safety standards
  • 4-point ratchet suspension system for comfortable all-day wear
  • UV protection coating prevents degradation from sunlight exposure
  • Adjustable sizing (52-64 cm) fits most head sizes
  • Ventilation slots for improved airflow
  • Lightweight design (350g) reduces neck strain
  • Compatible with face shields, earmuffs, and other PPE accessories

Applications:

  • Construction sites
  • Manufacturing facilities
  • Mining operations
  • Oil & gas industry
  • Warehousing & logistics
  • Electrical work

Safety Standards:

This helmet meets and exceeds the following safety certifications:

  • ISI (Indian Standards Institute) - IS 2925:1984
  • EN397 - European Standard for Industrial Safety Helmets
  • ANSI Z89.1 - American National Standards Institute

Maintenance Instructions:

  • Clean regularly with mild soap and water
  • Inspect for cracks or damage before each use
  • Replace every 3-5 years or after any impact
  • Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Do not paint or apply stickers (can weaken the shell)
