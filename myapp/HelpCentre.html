Account & Login

Click on "Sign Up" at the top right of the homepage. Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. Your account is ready to use!

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your email (including spam folder) and follow the link to reset your password.

Payment & Refunds
